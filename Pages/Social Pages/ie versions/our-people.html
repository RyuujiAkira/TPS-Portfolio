  • Diversity

    At The Perfume Shop we celebrate diversity and we're on a conscious journey to ensure that we provide an inclusive and equitable workplace where everyone can be themselves and feel included.

    Last year we launched our Reverse Mentoring scheme where a number of Gen Z TPS People paired up with our Leadership Team & Senior Management Team to see what they could learn from each other. From understanding more about shopping habits to Tik Tok creations, we’ve seen some brilliant connections and learnings so far for both mentors and mentees.

  • Discover Your Talents

    We love “Growing our Own” through learning and development at The Perfume Shop and aim to create a stimulating environment where our teams can flourish. In 2021, through our interactive employee accessible learning platform DISCOVER, there were 317 pieces of new learning created, 119,000 modules completed and over 900 competition prizes won. We are proud to have over 400 Fragrance Foundation Experts in our stores, with our top scoring exam takers celebrated at the Fragrance Foundation Awards. During 2022 we will continue to deliver a blended learning experience, via face-to-face meetings, online modules and our learner app so we can help our Perfume People develop their talent, passion and spark and grow within our business.

  • Reward

    We recognise and reward every individual for their contribution to The Perfume Shop. There are plenty of benefits and perks available, including Celebration Days - where our perfume people are able to take an additional day off in celebration of a wedding, new home or big birthday - on top of our holiday allowance, generous staff discount, discretionary bonus, store incentives, pension as well as access to The Retail Trust (Employee Assistance Programme).

  • Staying Connected

    Now more than ever, it's important all employees have a way of connecting with our business. Our internal communications platform “The Nose” enables teams to connect 24/7 and keep up to date on all things The Perfume Shop. In 2021, we posted over 550 articles, saw over 1,700 employee posts to our app, and had 580,000 content views.
